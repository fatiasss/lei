The Story of the Olympic Games

The Olympic Games have a rich history dating back to ancient Greece. Originally a festival in honor of Zeus, the modern Olympics were revived in 1896 by Pierre de Coubertin. Today, the Games bring nations together in a celebration of sport, unity, and excellence.
